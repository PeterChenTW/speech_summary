Summary:
The speaker at the semiconductor conference discussed the challenges and advancements in smart manufacturing. They emphasized the need for improved technologies and methods to meet customer specifications and reduce costs. They highlighted the importance of accurate measurements in manufacturing, especially as dimensions become smaller. They also discussed the role of machine learning in making quick and accurate decisions based on data collected from various sources. They shared examples of how machine learning has improved efficiency and accuracy in their work, such as reducing the number of manual clicks from 170,000 to 1,000 per month and speeding up a 40-minute measurement to under a minute. They concluded by emphasizing the importance of data sharing in enabling smart manufacturing.
